Biography

I am a PhD Student specialized in Digital Humanities, Artificial Intelligence, and exploring how computational methods can enhance and transform humanities scholarly research.

My research interests include the integration of Multimodal Large Language Models with large-scale image datasets of humanities content. Other areas include Natural Language Processing, Entity Linking, and Semantic Web technologies, with a focus on Linked Data for cultural heritage.

My work centers on knowledge discovery and developing processes for mining, exploring, categorizing, and visualizing humanities data, and creating digital infrastructures that support authoring and publication of research activities.
